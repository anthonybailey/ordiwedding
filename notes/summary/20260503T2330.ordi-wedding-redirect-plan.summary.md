## Decisions

- **Let `ordi.wedding` expire on 2026-05-28** (5 days after the wedding). The cheaper `ordiwedd.ing` is retained as the sole domain.
- **Before expiry, add a 301 redirect** from `ordi.wedding` → `ordiwedd.ing` so any lingering bookmarks survive past expiry (until the registrar releases the domain).
- **Redirect goes in the Cloudflare Worker, not on monkeys.** Reason: monkeys only sees `/-/*` traffic; the homepage `/` goes to Canva and never reaches Apache. The Worker is the only tier that sees all traffic for both domains. (Anthony's initial suggestion was monkeys; pushed back, agreed.)
